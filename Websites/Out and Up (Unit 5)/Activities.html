Adventure Course

Our adventure course has many things to do, parts of it are like a military training course. Our instructors are skilled to get you through if you do get stuck. In another section we have a rock climb or a rope climb to race to the top, after you get there there is a 100m long zip wire to the bottom! The course is suitable for all different difficulty and skill levels. As seen on our homepage we have a high wire race. We run tournaments on this often. The winners get cash prizes. The age catogorys for this are 11-14, 15-16, 17-18, 18+
